Privacy Policy

Protecting learner data across every institution

This policy explains what information Money Blocks collects, how we use it to run the simulation platform, and the controls institutions have to keep their cohorts isolated.

Back to site Contact us

Information We Collect

We collect only the data required to operate the simulation and verify access per institution.

  • Account details such as name, email, and institution affiliation.
  • Authentication data (Google domain restriction or local credentials).
  • Simulation activity like orders placed, portfolio values, and scenario participation.
  • Technical diagnostics including IP address, device type, and browser version for security.

How We Use Data

Data is used to deliver the platform, maintain institutional isolation, and improve reliability.

  • Provision and secure access to institution-specific portals.
  • Enforce manager controls such as trading windows, short limits, and crisis drills.
  • Provide support, troubleshooting, and service alerts.
  • Analyze aggregated, de-identified usage to improve the simulation experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Sharing is limited to trusted vendors necessary to run the service.

  • Infrastructure and security providers that host and protect the platform.
  • Authentication providers used for domain-restricted sign-in.
  • Compliance or legal requests when required by law.

Cookies and Similar Technology

We use cookies and local storage to keep users signed in, remember institution context, and secure sessions. Browser settings allow you to control or block cookies, but doing so may limit portal access.

Retention and Deletion

Account and simulation records are retained for as long as an institution maintains an active portal. When a portal is closed, data is scheduled for deletion or de-identification unless required for legal, security, or auditing purposes.

Security

Money Blocks uses encryption in transit, hashed passwords for local accounts, signed sessions, and least-privilege access controls. Each institution’s data remains isolated and is never commingled with other portals.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information subject to institutional requirements. Contact your program lead or email us to start a request.

hello@money-blocks.org

Updates

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. We will revise the “Last updated” date and, when changes are material, provide notice within institutional portals or via email.
